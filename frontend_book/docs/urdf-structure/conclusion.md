# Summary and Next Steps

## What We've Covered

In this book, you've learned the fundamentals of ROS 2 for Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics:

1. **ROS 2 Fundamentals**: Understanding the core concepts of ROS 2 and why it's essential for humanoid robots
2. **Communication Model**: Mastering nodes, topics, services, and the agent-controller communication flow
3. **URDF Structure**: Creating detailed robot descriptions using URDF for simulation, control, and digital twins

## Applying Your Knowledge

Now that you understand these concepts, you can:

- Create detailed URDF descriptions for your own humanoid robots
- Set up proper communication patterns between different robot subsystems
- Integrate your robot with simulation environments
- Build control systems that leverage the robot's structure

## Further Learning

To continue your journey with ROS 2 and humanoid robotics:

- Explore advanced control techniques like inverse kinematics
- Learn about robot simulation with Gazebo or other physics engines
- Study perception systems for humanoid robots
- Investigate motion planning algorithms
- Understand robot middleware and real-time considerations
